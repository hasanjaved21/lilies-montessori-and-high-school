About Us

Our Story & Mission

Three decades of nurturing minds and shaping the future of Pakistan's youth.

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1990

Lilies Montessori and High School was founded with a singular purpose — to make quality education accessible to every child, regardless of background. Since our founding in Karachi, we have grown into one of the city's most respected secondary institutions.

Our motto, "Truth and Love", is not just a slogan — it is the principle that has guided every decision we have made for over 30 years. We have produced thousands of graduates who have gone on to excel in medicine, engineering, law, and public service.

Today, Lilies Montessori and High School serves over 1,200 students across Grades VI through X, with a dedicated faculty of more than 65 qualified educators committed to the highest teaching standards.

🎯 Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school in Pakistan that produces ethical, knowledgeable, and confident citizens ready for the 21st century.

📖 Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, inclusive secondary education that fosters critical thinking, moral values, and lifelong learning in every student.

🌟 Our Values

Honesty, Discipline, Respect, Perseverance, and Community — these core values shape our culture inside and outside the classroom.

Step-by-Step Process

How to Apply

1

Visit the School or Enquire Online

Come to our admissions office or fill out the enquiry form below. We'll provide you with all details about available classes, fee structure, and curriculum options.

2

Submit Application Form & Documents

Complete the admission form and submit photocopies of Birth Certificate, previous report cards, National Identity Card (parent's), and 4 passport photos.

3

Entry Test & Interview

Students sit a short written entry test in English, Urdu, and Mathematics. A brief parent-teacher meeting follows to discuss the student's background and expectations.

4

Offer Letter & Fee Payment

Successful candidates receive an offer letter within 5 working days. Once the registration fee is paid, the seat is confirmed and the welcome package is issued.
